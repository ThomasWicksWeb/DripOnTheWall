Why Drip
on the
Wall?

  • We Do Not Use Subcontractors

    Here at Drip On the Wall our philosophy is based on establishing proper rapport, and a relationship with our clients that exceeds expectations. For that reason, we do not use any subcontractors or third-party companies. Instead, all personnel working on your project will be trained, backed, and employed by Drip On the Wall. Prior to the start of your project, you will receive the name and a short profile of the representative that will be working with you.

  • We Are a Smoke Free Company

    We pride ourselves on treating your house or business just like our own. This is why Drip On the Wall enforces a strict no-smoking policy to ensure an environment free of smoke.

  • We Are Licensed, Bonded, and Insured

    Drip On the Wall takes great pride in being a licensed, insured, and bonded company. We want you to be comfortable and confident with allowing us to complete your project.

  • We Respect Your Time

    We understand that you may need your project completed by a certain date. Whether it’s for family that is arriving this weekend, or for a business meeting taking place this week, we do our best to ensure your project is completed in a timely manner that works best to fit your needs. This is why we offer you the ability to get a same-day quote if you call before 12pm, and we can even begin your project the very next day if you’d like to! If you are unable to call before 12pm you may still receive a next-day quote if you submit for a free quote before 12am.

  • We Provide Financial Flexibility

    Drip On the Wall wants to make every step of your project as convenient as possible. This also includes convenience in completing the payment for your project. For this reason, we are happy to offer financing options to all of our customers.
