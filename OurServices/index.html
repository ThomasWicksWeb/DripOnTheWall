Our Services

Start out right

Here at Drip On the Wall we believe in making your next painting project a creative and stress-free task. Which is why we offer the widest range of services to help you complete any painting project, big or small. From interior painting to exterior painting, from color consultation to garage painting, we do it all! Don’t believe us? Just take a look at our full list of services:

  • Interior Painting

    The color in any room has more influence than you may think. It has the power to drastically change the mood in the room and can even make the room appear more expansive or smaller. Therefore, it is important that you have your interior household painted the way that best expresses who you are.

    Drip On the Wall is here to help paint any part of your interior household. Whether it’s the living room, bedroom, kitchen, laundry room, or even the basement; we’ve got you covered.

  • Exterior Painting

    The exterior of your house is the first thing people see when they look at your house. It serves as an expression of who you are and gives a preview of what's inside.

    Make sure the exterior of your house is the best it can be by freshening it up with a new color or by adding a new coat of the current color. Regardless of what you do, Drip On the Wall will make sure anyone who passes your house is amazed.

  • Color Consultation

    Believe it or not, but there is a whole science behind colors. The color of any object or environment can drastically change our perception of something. Color is a powerful thing and with so many, picking a color can be an extremely stressful process.

    However, there is no need to worry. Drip On the Wall is fluent in all things color. We will walk with you every step of the way in picking your color that best suits your needs and desires.

  • Fence Painting & Staining

    Whether it’s your brand new fence or an old one that needs a touch-up, we’ve got your back. A fresh coat of paint on your old fence can transform the look of your backyard, and can give your front yard a bold look. Drip On the Wall can even stain your fence for you. A fresh stain on your wood fence will add bold color while keeping the natural grain look of your fence.

  • Porch/Patio/Deck Painting & Staining

    A brand new look on any porch can make those afternoons sitting on your porch even sweeter. Let us give you the confidence to host your family barbeque with your brand new patio. It all starts with some fresh paint.

    Drip On the Wall can even paint your pool deck and even your driveway. So no matter, Drip On the Wall can help you spruce up any part of your backyard or front yard. Oh, and did we mention we can paint surface? Wood, concrete, you name it and we can do it.

  • Garage Painting

    Just cleaned out your garage and want to add some new color to it? Drip On the Wall is here to help. We can help you bring new color and life to your garage walls and even floors. Come home to a newly painted garage.
